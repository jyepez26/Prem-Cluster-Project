Player Type 3

Creative Conductors

Type 3 Players:

Mohamed Salah, Matheus Cunha, Jarrod Bowen, Son Heung-min, Bukayo Saka, Justin Kluivert, Anthony Elanga, Anthony Gordon

These players are primarily wingers who are not only incredibly clinical, but also irreplaceable creators on their teams. With both pace and fearlessness, they are not afraid to take an unexpected shot from far or put a fullback in the blender and put the ball on a red carpet for their teammates to find the back of the net. They love to whip the ball into dangerous areas of the box as well. Funnily enough, most of these players are left-footed.
